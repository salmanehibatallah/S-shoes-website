Our History

Born from a passion for craftsmanship, Salmane Chaussures was founded in 2006 in Morocco with a single workshop and a bold ambition: to bring European-quality shoes to market at competitive manufacturing costs. In nearly two decades, we grew from 10 artisans to a 300-person operation without losing what made us special — attention to every stitch.

Experience & Know-How

We work with brands across France, Spain, Portugal, and the UK — from emerging designers to established labels. Our team masters every technique: Goodyear welt, Blake stitch, cemented construction, and bespoke finishing. Whatever your design demands, we have the skills to execute it precisely.

Our Vision

We believe Morocco is the future of responsible, European-quality manufacturing of shoes. Our vision: to be the preferred partner for brands that want quality, transparency, and a supplier they can actually visit in a single afternoon flight from Paris or Madrid.
